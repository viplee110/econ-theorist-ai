# Full Walkthrough Toy Project

This toy project demonstrates the whole workflow without claiming a real
economic contribution.

Toy idea:

> A team produces a joint output. Individual effort is privately costly, and
> public recognition may arrive before or after effort is chosen. Early
> recognition can motivate effort, but it can also redirect effort toward visible
> tasks that are not the most productive for the team.

Workflow path:

```text
00 rough idea
01 literature evidence ledger
02 field and target profiles
03 primitive hunter
04 model base design
05 theorem and absorption
06 working preview note
07 style, review, and revision decision
```

Important caveat:

The literature entries are placeholders that show record structure. A real
project must replace them with verified sources opened by the assistant or
provided by the user.
